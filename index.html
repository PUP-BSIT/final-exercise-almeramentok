Journal Entry

December 30, 2023

In five years, I aspire to be knowledgeable about my passion. Currently, I am uncertain about my ultimate dream, but I envision myself in a happy and comfortable place. I acknowledge that life can be challenging, especially in the demanding field of IT where stress is prevalent. It might sound humorous, but my ambition is to achieve success as quickly as possible.
I aim to nurture my inner child and provide my mother with the fulfilling life she deserves. There is a sense of urgency in my quest, as I regret not being able to offer my father the best life he deserved. Over the past few months, I've faced numerous breakdowns, causing me to question the value of my academic performance. Despite these doubts, I hope that in the upcoming weeks, I can reaffirm my worth in this industry.
I want these next few weeks to serve as a renewed motivation to pursue a career in this field. Although I have a desire to be here, I am still uncertain about the specific type of work I want to pursue after graduating.
